# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Critique ideas, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on the repository.
- Respect privacy, consent, and boundaries.
- Assume good intent and verify facts before escalating.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Threats, intimidation, or sabotage
- Spam, trolling, or repeated disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues or pull requests, and revoke participation privileges when necessary to protect the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through GitHub or another maintainer channel. Do not use public issues for sensitive reports.
