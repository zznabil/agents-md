# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors and maintainers to behave professionally and respectfully.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Respect boundaries, privacy, and consent.
- Keep communication focused on the work.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Threats, intimidation, or deliberate sabotage
- Spam, trolling, or repeated disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues or pull requests, and revoke participation privileges when needed to protect the project.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through GitHub or another maintainer channel. Do not use public issues for sensitive conduct reports.
